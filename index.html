Discover & Interact

You will find a comprehensive set of information and interactive tools to perform real-time monitoring of real estate markets both at the national and regional level in the UK. The analyses provided relate to sustainability of house prices, affordability, and uncertainty in the housing market. In addition, we make available to you a new price index that is disaggregated up to level NUTS 3 such that you can perform your own analysis. This webpage provides an interactive platform to access these statistics.
